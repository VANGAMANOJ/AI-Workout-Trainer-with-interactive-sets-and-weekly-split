AI Workout Trainer

Intelligent fitness coaching powered by computer vision

🎯

Purpose

AI Workout Trainer is built to make personal fitness coaching accessible to everyone. Using real-time camera analysis and AI-driven feedback, it corrects your form, counts your reps, and tracks your progress — no gym trainer required.

🧠

How It Works

The app uses your device camera to detect body keypoints in real time. It calculates joint angles to verify form accuracy, counts repetitions automatically, and provides immediate feedback on technique corrections.

📋

Features

  • Real-time pose estimation via camera
  • Rep counter with joint angle tracking
  • 7-day personalized workout plans
  • 10 fitness goal categories
  • Goal-based diet recommendations
  • PDF export of full fitness plan
  • Dark & light theme support
🚀

Technology

Built with vanilla JavaScript and the Web Camera API. No backend required — all processing happens locally in your browser for maximum privacy. Designed for Vercel deployment with zero build steps.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to intelligent, personalized fitness guidance. AI Workout Trainer democratizes coaching by bringing AI-powered form analysis directly to your browser — free, private, and always available.
